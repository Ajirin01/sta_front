REQUIREMENT AND PROCEDUERS FOR ADMISSION

The school aims at providing the most excellent standard of education that will lay the solid foundation for the spiritual, moral, socio-cultural development and academic advancement of the students. We focus on making consecrated hearts, educated minds and equipped hands. This will enable the students to develop character, especially in respecting godly principles and enjoying a daily walk with God here and in the hereafter.

  • Booking is done by picking-up an application form from the school afterpaying a non-refundable amount of N1,000.
  • Completed Application Form is submitted accompanied by the necessary documents as stated on the application form
  • Attend an interview/chat (both parents and pupil) with the Admission Committee.
  • Diagnostic/placement test will be conducted on the child.
  • Upon acceptance of the placement result; all fees are paid in the bank and teller exchanged for receipt in the school.
  • Upon payment of required fees, parents attend a scheduled Parents Orientation in the school in preparation for resumption of the pupil.
